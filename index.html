About her

Waai Fu is a university student from Lungmen, serving Rhodes Island from the first batch of employees hired under a secret Lungmen-Rhodes Island commercial agreement. On the recommendation of a certain firm, she took a battery of tests that showed scores wildly inconsistent with her stated identity. She clearly has substantial combat experience. Several investigations are ongoing.

Fewer than 600 Sumatran tigers are estimated to remain in the wild. They are listed as Critically Endangered on the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species due to poaching, habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict. The Sumatran tiger is found only on the Indonesian island after which it is named.

An adult Sumatran tiger weighs 260 pounds on average and measures up to 8-feet long. They are actually the smallest of the tiger subspecies. Other species of tigers can weigh up to 660 pounds and can get up to 10-feet long.

”
Sumatran tigers have the narrowest black stripes of any tiger subspecies and they're also the darkest in orange compared to other subspecies. No two tigers have the exact same set of markings, making them identifiable by their uniquely patterned coats.
